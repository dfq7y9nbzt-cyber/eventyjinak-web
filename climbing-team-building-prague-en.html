Team building for teams from Prague

Corporate climbing team building for teams from Prague and the surrounding area with a real rock programme.

We prepare outdoor and climbing events for teams, management groups and agencies from Prague and the surrounding area who want something stronger than a standard programme in a camp or on a field. Transport, instructors, safety, equipment and logistics stay in one solution.

Who it is for

For companies that want an authentic experience and not improvisation.

  • groups from 3 to 40+ people depending on programme and venue
  • management and executive groups
  • event agencies looking for a strong outdoor module
  • foreign groups that expect smooth service

What we arrange

The client gets one complete solution from first brief to final delivery.

Real climbers

The event is led by people from real climbing practice who know the rock, the gear, belaying and group work in terrain.

Transport from Prague and the surrounding area

Depending on the event type, we handle transport to and from the venue so the client does not need separate logistics.

Safety-led delivery

We always adapt the programme to participant experience, venue, weather and current conditions.

Service for companies

Itinerary, budget, communication, refreshments, photo coverage and further add-ons are handled centrally and without chaos.

Typical setup

How this team building format usually looks

  • groups from 3 to 40+ people depending on programme, venue and instructor count
  • one-day or weekend format
  • beginner-friendly and mixed-experience groups
  • rock programme, team elements and supporting service

Final pricing always depends on group size, venue, transport and the selected service scope.

Useful to know in advance

Even before the first inquiry we can tell you what we need and how we build the event.

What we need before pricing

Group size, rough timing, experience level, expected intensity of the programme and whether transport, refreshments, photo or accommodation should be included.

How we choose instructors

Events are delivered with people from climbing practice. We match instructors to the venue, group size, event format and the level of responsibility needed on site.

Who this may not be ideal for

If you are looking only for the cheapest possible programme without transport, food and wider service options, we are probably not the right fit.
