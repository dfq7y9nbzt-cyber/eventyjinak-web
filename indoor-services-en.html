Indoor services

Climbing programmes in Prague for companies, organisations and groups.

If you want a complete programme for your company or group, but do not want to travel outside Prague, we can prepare an indoor climbing event around your group size and preferences. We choose a suitable climbing gym, arrange entry, rental equipment, instructors, on-site briefing and belaying.

Looking for a complete service, an unusual team building format and the comfort of staying in Prague? The indoor option is easier to plan, less dependent on weather and logistically simpler, while still being built around real climbing, instructors and full event organisation. If needed, we can add catering, a simple animation programme, a team game, an escape-style activity with real climbing or a photographer to document the event.
