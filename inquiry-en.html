Non-binding inquiry

Ask for the date and experience that fits you best.

Sending this form creates only a non-binding inquiry. We confirm real availability of instructors, rock area or indoor climbing gym, transport when needed and capacity individually. A binding reservation is created only after we send the contract draft and the deposit payment is received.

How the inquiry works

Share the key details and we prepare a real event proposal with suitable date options.

1.

You choose the event type or describe your own request.

2.

You add dates, group size, experience level and specific needs.

3.

We check instructors, rock area or indoor gym, transport and possible add-ons.

4.

Within 3 business days we respond with the next realistic option.

5.

We can also suggest a weather backup version of the event.

6.

After approval we send a contract draft, terms, a 50% deposit invoice and any follow-up questionnaire if needed.

7.

The reservation becomes binding only after the deposit reaches our account. Any later cancellation then follows the cancellation terms.
