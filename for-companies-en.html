For companies

Corporate climbing events outdoors and indoors with professional organisation.

Eventy Jinak is built for companies, event agencies, foreign groups and organisers who are looking for corporate team building in Prague, outdoor rock events, an indoor climbing programme at a Prague climbing gym or a real climbing experience without an amateur feel and without complicated coordination on their side.

How cooperation works

We keep the process simple, but professional.

1. Brief and assignment

We confirm group size, experience level, timing, target format and what the event should deliver to the client.

2. Proposal

We suggest a suitable rock area or indoor climbing gym, logistics, programme scope, instructor setup and any useful add-on services.

3. Confirmation and contract

Once the proposal is approved, we prepare the offer, contract structure and deposit-based date confirmation.

4. Event delivery

On site we hold the programme flow, communication and safety supervision so the client does not need to manage operations.
