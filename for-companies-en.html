For companies

Climbing and outdoor events for company clients who want the real thing, not a generic programme.

Eventy Jinak is built for companies, event agencies, foreign groups and organisers who want an authentic rock-based experience with professional structure and full service around it.

What that means

We do not just sell an activity. We prepare a usable event format from first brief to safe return.

The project combines real climbers and real management discipline. That means authentic outdoor delivery on one side and clear planning, communication and structure on the other.

Who this is for

  • companies looking for unusual team building
  • small management or executive groups
  • event agencies looking for a strong outdoor module
  • foreign groups and travel partners
  • private organisers with higher expectations on service

What clients can rely on

The important parts that need to work before the group even arrives in the rocks.

Authentic leadership

The programme is shaped by people from climbing practice, not by generic event entertainers. Rock terrain and gear are real working environments for us.

Safety structure

Each event is built according to venue, group experience, weather and the difficulty of the terrain. Safety is part of the concept from the beginning.

Clear process

From the first brief through the programme proposal to contract confirmation, the event stays in a simple and reliable workflow.

Complete service

Transport, equipment, itinerary, refreshments, photography and partner coordination stay in one system instead of falling apart into separate suppliers.

How cooperation works

Simple for the client, properly organised on our side.

1. Brief

We confirm group size, event goals, timing, client expectations and how strong or accessible the experience should be.

2. Proposal

We suggest the venue, logistics, programme scope, instructor setup and any add-on services that make sense.

3. Confirmation

Once the proposal is approved, we prepare the final offer, contract logic and deposit-based date confirmation.

4. Delivery

On site we hold the programme flow, communication and safety supervision so the client does not need to manage operations.

What we need from the client

  • group size and participant profile
  • date or at least a workable time window
  • goal and desired intensity of the event
  • whether transport, catering, photo or accommodation should be included
  • rough budget or price boundaries
