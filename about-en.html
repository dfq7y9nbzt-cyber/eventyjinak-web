About us

Real climbers, professional organisation and experiences people remember.

Eventy Jinak was created as an idea that connects climbing, outdoor movement and rock-based courses with events for companies, organisations and private groups that want a complete solution with the authentic spirit of rocks and nature.

Who we are and why we do this

We are real climbers from the rocks.

Climbing, belaying, rappelling, movement in rock terrain, equipment handling and leading people outdoors are a natural environment for us, not just scenery for an event. We build on real climbing experience, respect for rock areas and the ability to lead a group safely, calmly and with a sense of humour.

At the same time, we bring experience in management, client communication and full-day event organisation from the first brief to the safe return. That is our strength: authentic outdoor delivery from people in practice, led professionally and systematically.

We believe a corporate event, team building day or private outdoor experience does not have to be an artificial programme on a field or in a camp. It can be a properly prepared day in rock areas and nature, led by people who truly understand it and can deliver the whole service without chaos or improvisation.

What makes us different

We combine real climbing know-how with professional service for demanding clients.

  • Authentic people from practice We are not marketers or generic entertainers, but real people from climbing and outdoor practice.
  • Professional organisation We build on experience in leadership, management and day-to-day operational coordination.
  • Complete service from A to Z We want to give clients a truly complete solution so they do not have to solve the details themselves.
  • Strong solution for companies and agencies We focus on company clients, travel agencies and private group organisers.
