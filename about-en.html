About us

Real climbers, professional organisation and experiences people remember.

Eventy Jinak was created to connect two worlds: the world of climbing, outdoor movement and rock-based learning, and the world of professional event delivery for companies, organisations and private groups that want convenient full service without losing the authentic spirit of climbing and the outdoors.

Authenticity

Real rocks, real terrain and real climbing practice.

Complete service

Outdoor programmes organised professionally from first brief to final delivery.

Who we are

We are people from the rocks, not a generic event catalogue.

Climbing, belaying, rappelling, movement in rock terrain, equipment handling and leading people outdoors are a natural environment for us, not just visual scenery for an event. We build on real climbing experience, respect for rock areas and the ability to lead a group safely and calmly.

At the same time, through management, organisation, leadership and communication experience, we know how to build a service that works for companies, agencies, travel partners and foreign clients who want a complete solution from A to Z.

Why it works

Authentic outdoor delivery with a systematic professional layer.

That combination is what makes the project different. The outdoor part is led by real climbers and instructors from practice, while the event side is led with structure, clear communication, planning discipline and client care.

The result is not a game in a camp or a generic programme on a field. It is a real outdoor or climbing experience, professionally arranged and comfortably delivered for the client.
