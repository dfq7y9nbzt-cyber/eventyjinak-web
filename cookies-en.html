Information about cookies

This page explains what cookies or similar technical tools the Eventy Jinak website currently uses and why.

Current setup as of 30 April 2026
Download Czech DOCX Back to documents

1. What we currently use

The current website is primarily static and uses only technical elements needed for page display, navigation and form operation. At the moment, we do not knowingly run a broad advertising or profiling cookie setup.

2. Why no consent banner is shown

Because the current setup is focused on technical website operation and does not rely on marketing or advanced analytics cookies, the website does not currently require a full consent banner in the same way as an ad-driven website would.

3. What may change later

If we later introduce analytics, advertising pixels or third-party marketing tools, this page and the overall cookie handling will be updated accordingly.
