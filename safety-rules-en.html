Safety

Safety rules and risk acknowledgement

This document summarises the main rules and typical risks for participants in climbing, outdoor and experience-based Eventy Jinak activities.

1. What the participant confirms

  • that they have been clearly informed about the nature of the programme and its basic risks,
  • that they are in a physical and mental condition suitable for participation,
  • that they have disclosed or will disclose any important health limitations or allergies,
  • that they will follow the instructions of the instructor or other authorised person.

2. Core safety rules

  • follow the instructions of the instructor, guide or authorised organiser,
  • do not use equipment in a way other than instructed,
  • do not leave the group without permission,
  • do not participate under the influence of alcohol or drugs,
  • report illness, equipment failure or dangerous situations immediately.

3. Examples of risks the participant acknowledges

  • falling, slipping, tripping or impact against rock, equipment or another person,
  • falling stones, branches or other natural material,
  • injury during climbing, belaying, rappelling or gear handling,
  • weather changes, cold, heat, fatigue, stress or fear of heights,
  • risks connected to transport, movement in nature and accommodation.

4. Minors

For minors, consent from the legal guardian is required. The organiser may also request emergency contact details, health information and identification of the responsible adult.
